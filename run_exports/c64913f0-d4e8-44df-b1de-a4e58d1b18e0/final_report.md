# M&A research — coming in V2

The Mergers & Acquisitions consultant ships a full pipeline in **V2**.
For V1 this task is a skeleton: selecting it produces this placeholder
report so you can verify routing end-to-end without exercising the
research stack.

## What V2 will add

- **Deal thesis** — capture the strategic rationale (scale, capability,
  geography, defensive) and translate it into screening criteria.
- **Target sourcing** — query public M&A databases plus your own
  documents for candidates matching the thesis.
- **Triangulated valuation** — multiples, DCF, and precedent
  transactions, each with its own evidence trail.
- **Synergy & integration model** — revenue, cost, and dis-synergy
  projections with explicit assumptions.
- **IC-ready memo** — final synthesis stitched to citations, with an
  audit pass identical to the Market Entry workflow.
