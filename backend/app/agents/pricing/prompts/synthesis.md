Write the full Markdown body for `final_report.md` only. Do not include front matter or prose outside the report.

## Required sections in order

1. `# Pricing Strategy Report`
2. `## Executive Summary` - 5-8 bullets with the recommended pricing move, expected tradeoffs, and highest-risk assumptions.
3. `## Engagement Context` - objective, product or service scope, current model, customer segments, competitive context, target outcomes, and constraints.
4. `## Value & WTP` - customer value drivers, economic value, willingness-to-pay signals, and monetizable value gaps.
5. `## Segment Pricing` - price sensitivity by segment, value perception differences, packaging implications, and discount or fence logic.
6. `## Competitive Price Position` - benchmark set, competitor price and packaging comparisons, relative position, and competitor response risks.
7. `## Pricing Model Options` - evaluated models, fit criteria, shortlisted options, rejected options, and operational feasibility.
8. `## Pricing Recommendation & Rollout` - recommended structure, migration path, governance, enablement, risk mitigation, and rollout sequence.
9. `## KPI/Experiment Plan` - baseline, test design, success metrics, guardrails, owner, cadence, and decision thresholds.
10. `## Sources` - auto-generated; do NOT write this section yourself.

## Citation rules

- Do NOT invent new `src_id` values. Do NOT modify existing ones.
- Do NOT use numeric bracket citations, bare URLs, footnote reference sections, or a manually written sources section.
